how it works ?

It's very simple, the circle in the center of the screen grows for 4 seconds. As he grows, take a deep breath, calmly and deeply through your nose

Then it shrinks for 6 seconds. This is the time to release the pressure by exhaling calmly through your mouth. Feel the muscles in your body relax with each exhale.

This technique is ideal for calming down quickly.
